M.S.Dhoni In Indian Cricket

ODI Career

Dhoni scored 10,733 runs in ODIs ,including 10 centuries, at an average of 50.57.He captainedd India to victory in ODI World Cup, Champions Trophy, T20 World Cup

T20s

In T20 Internationals, DHoni scored 1,617 runs at an average of 37.60.His tactics played key-role in India's success during his captaincy.

IPL

Dhoni led CSK to five IPL titles(2010,2011,2018,2021 and 2023). Season 2018 was memeorable with 455 runs scored at strike 150.66

Wicket Keeping Skills

Dhoni's quick reflexes behind stumps made him one of the best wicketkeeper-batsmen in cricket history. His catch chances were remarkable with 89.7% in ODIs ,60.2% in T20s, and 57.7% in IPL.
